A11y note: IONOS Orange (#ff8800) on white has a contrast ratio of 2.39:1, below WCAG AA threshold of 4.5:1 for body text. This is acceptable on this preview because orange is used only on button backgrounds (paired with white text — see your DESIGN.md's "use orange only for UI elements ≥18px or as background-with-white-text" note) and as a brand accent. For body text and small UI, the deep secondary blue (#2944cc) is the safer choice.
